Types of Teas

Black Tea

Something interesting about black tea. Includes Earl Grey (black tea blend with bergamot) and Assam teas. Tea leaves are oxidized. How would you categorize oolong tea? Oolong tea leaves are only partially oxidized.

Green Tea

Something fascinating about green tea. Jasmine green tea is especially good. Tea leaves are not oxidized. Don't brew it too long or it gets bitter.

White Tea

Something intriguing about white tea. I don't know enough about white tea, but it's supposed to have the least caffeine and a more delicate taste.
